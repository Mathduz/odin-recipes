Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a traditional italian dish that is often remembered as one of the most italian foods, it consists basically in a pasta base with some tasteful fillings.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Layer the lasagna with one layer of noodle, then a layer of sauce
  4. Cover with foil and bake
